FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about bathtub reglazing, tile refinishing and our services in Queens, NY.

What is bathtub reglazing?

Bathtub reglazing (also called refinishing or resurfacing) is a process that restores the surface of your bathtub to a like-new appearance. A technician cleans the tub, repairs any chips or cracks, applies a bonding agent and then sprays on a new coating that looks and feels like a brand-new finish. It is a fraction of the cost of replacement and can be completed in a single day.

How long does reglazing last?

With proper care, a professional reglaze can last 10 to 15 years or more. We recommend using non-abrasive cleaners and avoiding harsh chemicals to preserve the finish. Regular maintenance and gentle cleaning will keep your reglazed surfaces looking great for many years.

How much does bathtub reglazing cost compared to replacement?

Bathtub replacement typically costs $1,000 to $5,000 or more including materials, disposal and installation. Professional reglazing costs a fraction of that. Most homeowners save 70–80% by choosing reglazing over replacement, making it one of the best value bathroom renovation decisions you can make.

Do you reglaze tiles and sinks too?

Yes! We offer full bathroom fixture refinishing including wall tile reglazing, sink reglazing, shower reglazing and cabinet refinishing. We can restore virtually any porcelain, ceramic, fiberglass or acrylic surface in your bathroom to a beautiful, durable finish.

How long before I can use my bathtub after reglazing?

We typically recommend waiting 24 hours after reglazing before using your bathtub. This allows the coating to fully cure and harden for maximum durability. Our technician will give you specific care instructions at the end of each job to make sure your new finish lasts as long as possible.
